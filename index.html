How to save & post

Get your reel onto Instagram

Your reel plays right here in the browser. To post it, you screen-record it once, then add music in Instagram. Takes about a minute.

01
Tap “Save your video”You’ll get simple on-screen steps and a 3-2-1 countdown.
02
Start your phone’s screen recordingiPhone: swipe into Control Centre and tap the record circle. Android: screen recorder in quick settings.
03
Let it play through onceIt plays a single time and stops. Then stop your recording.
04
Post to Instagram, add musicUpload the recording as a Reel and add a track from Instagram’s music library.
About

PropSight Studio

A free tool for Singapore property agents to turn listing photos into polished marketing videos for Instagram, WhatsApp and the portals. No editing skills, no apps.

Pick a format, pick a style, drop in your photos, and it builds the reel as you type. Every template keeps your photos true to colour and your details accurate.
